Top Engineering Colleges In Tamilnadu

The top Engineering colleges in Tamilnadu are IIT Madras, NIT Trichy, VIT Vellore, etc. Tamilnadu is home to around 954 Engineering colleges. There are 769 private and 125 Government colleges in Tamilnadu for Engineering. The best Engineering colleges in Tamilnadu offer courses in multiple streams including CSE, ECE, IT, etc. The top Engineering colleges in Tamilnadu

  • IIT Madras is the best Engineering college in Tamilnadu. It has been ranked 1st followed by NIT Trichy at 9th among the top Engineering colleges in India.
  • Admissions into the top engineering colleges in Tamil Nadu are based on Engineering entrance exams such as TNEA, JEE, GATE, TANCET, etc. followed by a GD/ PI round.
  • IIT Madras and Anna University are the top colleges in Chennai for Engineering which are ranked among the top 10 Engineering colleges in Tamilnadu.
  • The average fee for BTech courses in Tamil Nadu is between INR 1 to INR 3 lakh per annum, and for the MTech courses, the fee is between INR 50,000 to INR 1,00,000 per annum.
  • 500 Engineering colleges in Tamilnadu are affiliated with Anna University. PSGCT Coimbatore and CIT Chennai are the Best Engineering colleges in Tamilnadu under Anna University.
  • The top Engineering colleges in Tamilnadu offer good placement packages with high ROI. The average CTC offered ranges from around INR 7 to INR 12 LPA.

Top Engineering Colleges in Tamilnadu with NIRF Ranking 2023

NIRF or the National Institutional Ranking Framework releases the rankings of top colleges in India every year, divided into different categories. The table below shows the NIRF Engineering College Ranking list data.

  • IIT Madras has also been ranked as the 1st university all around India out of 200 participating universities.
  • The next top engineering college ranked by NIRF in Tamil Nadu is NIT Trichy which has secured the 9th rank in India.
